# Wealth tax
- One-off tax payable over 30 years funding EU sovereign fund which more than matches EU common debt so that dividends from the fund exceed debt repayment
- Pisani-Ferry & Mahfouz (23) recommend to raise 5% of GDP = 5% of financial wealth of top 10%. At EU level, that would be 900G€, i.e. twice common debt [Piketty proposes to raise 30% of GDP]
Schedules achieving 5% (assuming 15% depreciation and 20% evasion): 
- 0.1% > 1M, 0.25% > 10M, 0.5% > 100M
- 0.5% > 100M, 2% > 1G
